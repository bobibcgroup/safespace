## Suggested Enhancements

### 1. **Enhanced Sentiment Analysis**
**Current:** Basic percentages (30% positive, 25% neutral, 45% negative)

**Improvements:**
- **Sentiment Trends Over Time**: Track sentiment changes across response dates
- **Sentiment by Theme**: Show which themes are associated with positive/negative sentiment
- **Emotional Intensity**: Measure the strength of sentiment (mild vs. strong)
- **Sentiment Drivers**: Identify what specifically drives positive vs. negative sentiment
- **Comparative Sentiment**: Compare against previous campaigns or industry benchmarks

**Example Output:**
```
Sentiment Analysis:
- Overall: 30% positive, 25% neutral, 45% negative
- Trend: Declining (negative sentiment increased 15% from last quarter)
- Strongest Negative Driver: Workload distribution (mentioned in 8 negative responses)
- Strongest Positive Driver: Flexible work arrangements (mentioned in 6 positive responses)
```

### 2. **Advanced Theme Analysis**
**Current:** Basic themes with counts

**Improvements:**
- **Theme Clustering**: Group related themes together
- **Theme Sentiment Mapping**: Show sentiment distribution per theme
- **Theme Urgency Scoring**: AI-calculated urgency based on frequency, sentiment, and impact
- **Theme Relationships**: Identify which themes are often mentioned together
- **Theme Evolution**: Track how themes change over time
- **Actionable Theme Insights**: Specific recommendations per theme

**Example Output:**
```
Theme: Workload Distribution
- Frequency: 8 mentions (40% of responses)
- Sentiment: 87% negative, 13% neutral
- Urgency: HIGH
- Related Themes: Work-life balance, Team collaboration
- Key Insight: Uneven workload is causing burnout and resentment
- Recommended Action: Implement workload visibility dashboard
```
